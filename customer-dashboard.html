Frequently Asked Questions

Who can see my listing?

Only verified investors who have completed identity verification and signed NDAs can view your full listing details.

How does communication work?

All investor communication is handled through Clippit Admin to protect both parties. You'll receive notifications of inquiries and offers, and can respond through your dashboard.

What types of investments are supported?

We support equity funding, full buyouts, partnerships, and acquisitions. You choose the type when creating your listing.

How long does approval take?

Admin review typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your listing is approved or if any changes are needed.

Can I edit my listing after it's live?

Yes, you can edit your listing at any time. Major changes will require re-approval from admin.

What are the fees?

Listing is free. Clippit charges a success fee only when a deal closes, which will be discussed during the process.
